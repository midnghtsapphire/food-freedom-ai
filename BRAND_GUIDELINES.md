# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Position

Food Freedom AI is practical, supportive, and results-oriented. Messaging should emphasize confidence, personalization, and sustainable nutrition habits.

## Voice & Tone

- Clear and direct over technical jargon
- Encouraging, not judgmental
- Outcome-focused ("build your week faster", "eat with confidence")

## UX Principles

- Keep flows short from goal input to meal output
- Preserve trust with transparent auth, billing, and data handling
- Prioritize accessibility and mobile readability

## Visual Consistency

- Use existing design tokens and shadcn/ui patterns from the current app
- Keep spacing and hierarchy consistent with existing components

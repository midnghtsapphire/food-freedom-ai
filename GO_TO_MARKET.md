# Go-To-Market

## Product Value

Food Freedom AI helps users convert nutrition goals into practical meal plans and actionable shopping decisions, reducing planning friction and improving consistency.

## Priority Goal Alignment

- **Primary goal:** increase retention through personalized nutrition workflows
- **Business outcome:** convert free users to subscriptions through AI meal-planning value
- **Three-year target framing:** scale toward a sustainable, high-growth subscription business

## Core Audience

1. Busy professionals needing weekly meal automation
2. Health-focused users tracking macros and dietary constraints
3. Coaches and wellness communities seeking repeatable planning workflows

## Launch Motion

1. **Website in Test on Vercel** for rapid preview validation
2. Content-driven onboarding (nutrition use cases + meal templates)
3. Conversion path from free planning to premium subscription features
4. Feedback loop from analytics + support into product iteration

## KPIs

- activation: user creates first meal plan
- retention: weekly meal plan regeneration rate
- monetization: trial-to-paid conversion rate
- reliability: API uptime and checkout completion rate
